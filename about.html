About Life Receipt

Your life, quantified and visualized

What is Life Receipt?

Life Receipt is a fun, interactive web service that transforms your life statistics into a familiar convenience store receipt format. By inputting basic information like your birthdate, country, and lifestyle habits, you'll receive a unique "receipt" showing how you've spent your time on Earth.

From the cups of coffee you've consumed to the hours you've slept, Life Receipt gives you a fascinating perspective on your life's journey. It's designed to be shareable, thought-provoking, and above all - fun!

How It Works

1

Enter Your Information

Provide basic details like your birthdate, country, and optional lifestyle information.

2

Our algorithm processes your data using statistical averages and realistic assumptions.

3

Get Your Receipt

Receive a beautiful, shareable receipt showing your life statistics in an engaging format.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding how we spend our time can be both enlightening and entertaining. Life Receipt was created to provide a unique perspective on life's journey, encouraging reflection while remaining lighthearted and shareable. Whether you're amazed by how many cups of coffee you've consumed or surprised by your free time, we hope Life Receipt sparks interesting conversations and self-reflection.

Privacy & Data

All calculations happen in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to our servers. Your data stays on your device, ensuring complete privacy. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

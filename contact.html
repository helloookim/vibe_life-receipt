Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes! All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How accurate are the statistics?

Life Receipt uses statistical averages and general assumptions to provide entertaining estimates. Results are approximations and should not be considered exact measurements. See our Terms of Service for more information.

Can I share my Life Receipt?

Absolutely! Use the share button to download your receipt as an image and share it on social media. We love seeing Life Receipts in the wild!

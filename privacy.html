Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Introduction

Life Receipt ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our web service at lifereceipt.com.

Information We DON'T Collect

✅ Your Privacy is Protected

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information you enter into Life Receipt. All calculations are performed entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. Your birthdate, country selection, and lifestyle inputs never leave your device.

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do NOT store your birthdate or age
  • We do NOT track your country or location beyond basic analytics
  • We do NOT save your lifestyle preferences (coffee consumption, sleep hours, etc.)
  • We do NOT create user accounts or profiles

Information We MAY Collect

Like most websites, we may collect limited, anonymized analytics data to improve our service:

  • Anonymous Usage Statistics: Number of visitors, page views, and receipt generations (via Google Analytics or similar services)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, and general geographic region (country-level only)
  • Cookies: We may use cookies for analytics purposes only (see Cookies section below)

How Your Data is Processed

When you use Life Receipt:

  1. You enter your information in the web form
  2. JavaScript code running in YOUR browser performs all calculations locally
  3. Your "Life Receipt" is generated and displayed in YOUR browser
  4. If you choose to share your receipt, you manually save or share it using your device's built-in functions
  5. At no point is your personal data sent to our servers

Cookies

We may use cookies for:

  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site (via Google Analytics)
  • Language Preference: To remember your selected language (EN/KO)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • CDN Services: For faster content delivery

These services may collect their own data according to their privacy policies. We do not share any personal information with these services because we don't collect it in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Life Receipt is a general audience service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since all processing happens in the browser and we don't collect data, this service can be used by anyone safely.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal information, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your information at all times because it never leaves your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
